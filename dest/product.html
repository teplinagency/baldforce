Ingredients

  • aloe vera

    Nature’s wonder treatment, our organic aloe vera active ingredient helps to stimulate the growth of fibroblasts as well as accelerating skin regeneration. It also soothes the skin and reduces any inflammation.

  • aqua cacteen

    A prickly pear extract, aqua cacteen blocks stress markers being released from sensory nerve cells. This helps to soothe and calm irritated skin. It also carries exceptional moisturizing properties

  • panthenol / pro-vitamin b5

    Pro-vitamin B5 to penetrate down to the dermis and reduce the chances of skin inflammation. It also supports healing and accelerates epithelial regeneration as well as boosting your skin’s elasticity and firmness.

  • niacinamide

    Vitamin B3 to stimulate collagen synthesis and act as a skin barrier for regeneration. With anti-inflammatory and anti-acne properties, it also hydrates and improves the elasticity of your skin.

  • shea butter

    Smooths and softens the skin while soothing any irritation. It is also a natural UV filter and leaves a film on the skin to protect against weather and dryness.

  • grapeseed oil

    Essential fatty acids help with the reconstruction of your skin’s hypo-lipid layer and also improve elasticity.

  • enantia chlorantha bark extract / oleanolic acid

    Works to reduce the size of your pores and the effect of your skin’s luminosity as well as improving the overall texture of your skin. In addition, it helps prevent a shiny scalp.
